Learn about the Mission

Congratulations and welcome to the GraphGist Challenge. Just model your domain, use case or problem in a simple text file. It will be rendered as an interactive page in your browser. Submit your GraphGist to Neo4j to be eligible for prizes.

Best of luck on your mission.

Mission runs until Jan 20 2016.

May the graph be with you.

Categories

Open/Government Data and Politics

Give the Rebels an upper hand by telling the universase what the Galactic Senate has been up to.

Investigative Journalism

Collect facts and use graphs to get insight into how the world works.

Holidays

Track Santa's shortest path, or make it easier to do gift recommendations.

Sports and Recreation

Develop a graph-based ratings system for your favorite podracers in the Outer Rim Territories.

Public Web APIs

Mashup data from public web APIs.

Pop Culture

Graph your favorite people, events, and other things in pop culture: TV, movies, books, plays, and more!

a) No purchase necessary. A purchase does not improve your chances of winning. 3. DETERMINATION OF WINNER(S)

There will be one grand prize winner for the Contest and six category winners for each category in the Contest (i.e. Open/Government Data and Politics; Investigative Journalism; Holidays; Sports and Recreation; Public Web APIs; and Pop Culture). 6 participants will receive honorable mentions.

A Neo Technology panel of judges will determine the winning participant(s) in Neo Technology's sole discretion based on the following judging criteria (weighted equally): creativity, relevance to category, quality of written explanation and visual presentation, adherance to data modeling and cypher best practices, and thoroughness.

Odds of winning depend on the number of eligible entries received.

In the event of a tie, Neo Technology panel will re-judge the entries based on the judging criteria above.
