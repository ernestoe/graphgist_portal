Learn about the Mission

Congratulations and welcome to the GraphGist Challenge. Just model your domain, use case or problem in a simple text file. It will be rendered as an interactive page in your browser. Submit your GraphGist to Neo4j to be eligible for prizes.

Best of luck on your mission.

Mission runs from Dec 7 2015 - Jan 10 2016.

May the graph be with you.

Categories

Open/Government Data and Politics

Give the Rebels an upper hand by telling the universase what the Galactic Senate has been up to.

Investigative Journalism

Collect facts and use graphs to get insight into how the world works.

Holidays

Track Santa's shortest path, or make it easier to do gift recommendations.

Sports and Recreation

Develop a graph-based ratings system for your favorite podracers in the Outer Rim Territories.

Public Web APIs

Mashup data from public web APIs.

Pop Culture

Graph your favorite people, events, and other things in pop culture: TV, movies, books, plays, and more!

Rules

  • The contest runs from when it's first announced on the Neo4j Blog until January 15, 2016 @ 11:59:59 PST.
  • All submissions must be done through the GraphGist portal.
  • Submissions will be judged by the judges entirely at their discretion.
  • Winners will be announced on the Neo4j Blog by February 1, 2016 and prizes will be shipped by February 15, 2016.
  • If winner of Amazon Gift Card resides outside the US, the gift card will be awarded using the local Amazon site if possible to do using a US credit card. Otherwise, an alternate gift card will be distributed or different arrangements will be made at the sole discretion of Neo Technology.
  • Neo Technology employees and contractors are not eligible.
  • Prizes have no cash value.
  • All decisions are final.
